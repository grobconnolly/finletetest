About Finlete

We connect fans with the athletes they believe in

Based in San Diego, California — building a new asset class at the intersection of sports and finance.

Our mission

More than a fan, more than a ticket

Finlete is a sports investment platform that lets everyday fans buy equity stakes in the future MLB earnings of real baseball prospects. We think supporting an athlete should mean more than buying a jersey — it should mean being part of their journey, with skin in the game.

Every Finlete offering is SEC-regulated under Regulation Crowdfunding and intermediated by DealMaker Securities, LLC (FINRA/SIPC). That means investors get the same legal protections afforded to traditional crowdfunded offerings — and athletes get upfront capital to invest in their own development without surrendering control of their careers.

What we believe

Three principles

Fans deserve ownership

If you're going to spend your life rooting for someone, you should be able to share in their success.

Athletes deserve capital

A loan shouldn't be the only path to funding your development. Future-earnings financing is the modern alternative.

Regulation makes it real

Every offering runs through an SEC-registered broker-dealer. No shortcuts, no grey area.
