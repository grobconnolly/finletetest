Why I'm raising capital

Hi, my name is Tirso Ornelas and I’ve loved baseball for as long as I can remember. When I was 17, I signed with the San Diego Padres who play just over the Mexico-USA border from my hometown of Tijuana. Since then, I’ve worked hard every day to grow as a player.

I was blessed to be able to wear a Padres jersey for the first time in 2025, and I’m doing everything I can to get back to the big leagues and stay there. I’ve come a long way, but there’s still a lot of work to do.

This journey takes focus, sacrifice, and support. A few years ago, I was driving for Door Dash just to help pay the bills. I’m raising money to help with things off the field so I can stay focused on giving everything I’ve got on the field. I love San Diego, and I want to make the fans proud.

Thank you for believing in me. If I achieve my big league goals, you will be right there with me, sharing in the glory.

Tirso Ornelas
