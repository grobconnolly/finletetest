Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Finlete Funding, Inc. collects, uses, and shares information when you visit the Finlete website or use our services. This is a plain-language summary — specific obligations and disclosures applicable to securities offerings are governed by the documentation for each offering.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone number, and mailing address when you register or inquire.
  • Investment data: information required by our broker-dealer partner to process a purchase, including identity verification and accreditation status where applicable.
  • Usage data: pages visited, device and browser information, approximate location, and interactions with the site, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the service.
  • To process investments, verify identity, and comply with applicable securities and anti-money-laundering laws.
  • To communicate with you about offerings, updates, and investor matters.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues.

3. Sharing

We share information with (a) our broker-dealer partner DealMaker Securities, LLC and its affiliates to process investments; (b) service providers who help us operate the platform (hosting, analytics, email delivery); (c) regulators, law enforcement, and other parties where required by law; and (d) successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and measure marketing performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect functionality.

5. Your choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to object to or limit certain processing. To exercise these rights, email info@finlete.com.

6. Data retention and security

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above and to comply with our legal obligations. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information, though no system can be perfectly secure.

7. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions about this Policy? Email info@finlete.com or write to 350 10th Ave, Ste 1000, San Diego, CA 92101.
