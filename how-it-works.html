How it works

A new era for athletes and fans

Finlete lets you invest in a rising MLB prospect's career and share in their future earnings. Here's how every offering works.

01

Invest in a player's career

You purchase preferred shares in a Finlete offering tied to a specific athlete. Each share entitles you to a fixed percentage of that athlete's future MLB earnings. The process takes under three minutes and the investment is SEC-regulated through our broker-dealer partner.

02

Follow their journey

Track the athlete's path through minor-league updates, exclusive livestreams, interviews, and behind-the-scenes content. You're not a passive holder — you're part of a community cheering them to the majors.

03

Receive dividends

Once the athlete begins earning MLB income, dividends are paid to investors twice a year — within 30 days after the All-Star Game and within 30 days after the World Series. Everything is handled automatically.

Why athletes work with Finlete

Capital to invest in themselves

Athletes often choose future earnings contracts for two key reasons:

  1. Investment in their career. Upfront capital lets them access better training facilities, coaches, nutrition, and recovery — without financial stress distracting from development.
  2. Fans join the journey. Finlete connects each athlete to a community of investors who are literally rooting for their success — a deeper bond than any jersey or ticket.
The fine print, simplified

A few important things to know

SEC-regulated

Offerings are intermediated by DealMaker Securities, LLC, a registered broker-dealer and FINRA/SIPC member.

One-year lockup

Shares sold via Regulation Crowdfunding have a one-year lockup period before transfer under certain conditions.

Real risk

Early-stage investments are speculative and illiquid. Only invest what you can afford to lose, and read the offering documents in full.
