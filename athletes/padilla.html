Frequently asked questions

Finlete offers athlete-specific preferred stock issued by Finlete Funding, Inc. Each share entitles holders to a fixed percentage of the named athlete's future MLB earnings, on the terms described in the offering circular.

A future earnings contract gives one party capital today in exchange for a percentage of the counterparty's future earnings. In Finlete's case, covered earnings are MLB salaries and performance bonuses — sponsorships and off-the-field income are excluded.

Dividends begin once the athlete is generating MLB earnings that Finlete Funding is entitled to collect. Payouts are made twice per year — within 30 days of the All-Star Game and within 30 days of the World Series.

The payout term is defined in each offering circular. For this offering, the payout term is 25 years from the effective date.
