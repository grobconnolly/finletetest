Why I'm raising capital

Hi, I’m Aroon Escobar. I play third base in the minor leagues for the Philadelphia Phillies.

I grew up in Venezuela loving baseball. It’s been my dream since I was a kid to make it to the Major Leagues. I signed with the Phillies when I was 17 years old and ever since then I’ve been working hard every day to get better and chase that dream.

I’m raising money on Finlete to help cover the things I need off the field, like food, training, and staying healthy. Playing in the minors is a grind, and every bit of support helps me stay focused and ready.

If you invest, you’re part of this journey. And if I make it, we’ll share that success together.
